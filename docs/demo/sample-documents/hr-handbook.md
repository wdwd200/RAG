# HR Handbook Demo

This document is a fictional handbook for the Phase 6 evaluation demo. It does not describe any real company policy.

## Annual Leave

Full-time employees receive 10 working days of annual leave after completing one full year of service. Annual leave should normally be used within the current calendar year. Unused annual leave may be carried over until the end of the first quarter of the next year. Leave not used by March 31 of the next year expires unless a manager approved a written exception before the deadline.

## Sick Leave

Employees may request paid sick leave when they are unable to work because of illness. Sick leave longer than two consecutive working days requires a medical note or equivalent proof. Sick leave requests should be submitted in the attendance system within three working days after returning to work.

## Probation Period

New employees have a probation period of three months. During probation, managers should complete at least one midpoint feedback conversation and one final probation review. If the probation result is not approved, Human Resources must notify the employee in writing before the probation end date.

## Overtime And Time Off In Lieu

Overtime must be approved by the direct manager before the work is performed. Approved overtime can be converted into time off in lieu at a one-to-one ratio. Time off in lieu should be used within 60 calendar days after the overtime date.
