# Expense Policy Demo

This document is a fictional expense policy for the Phase 6 evaluation demo. It does not contain real company financial rules.

## Travel Reimbursement Materials

Travel reimbursement must include the travel approval record, itinerary, transportation receipts, hotel invoice, and a short trip purpose summary. Missing approval records must be explained in the reimbursement note and approved again by the department manager.

## Invoice Requirements

Invoices must show the correct company title, tax number, amount, date, and item description. Handwritten receipts are accepted only for local transportation under 100 yuan when an electronic invoice is unavailable. Altered invoices or invoices without a clear seller name are rejected.

## Reimbursement Deadline

Employees should submit reimbursement within 30 calendar days after the expense date. For business trips, the deadline is 30 calendar days after the return date. Requests submitted after the deadline require finance manager approval.

## Approval Flow

Normal reimbursement is reviewed by the direct manager first and then by Finance. Reimbursement above 5,000 yuan requires department head approval before Finance review. Finance may return a request for correction if required materials are missing.
